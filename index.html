TokoVOIP 1.5
TokoVOIP is our in-game VOIP replacement, using a custom made TS Plugin.

It gives us a lot more possibilities, which currently allow such things as:
  • Better voice quality
  • Radio system
  • Proximity chat unique to each player
  • Phone calls
  • Radio clicks
  • Radio effects
TokoVOIP has to be installed and running to play on our servers.

You can find more information and download links at: https://voip.itokoyamato.net/
